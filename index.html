Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 May 2026

This app, Surah Player (package com.qurangen.app), is a simple offline audio player for Surah Ar‑Rahman. It does not collect, store, or share any personal information, device data, or usage statistics.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any data. The app works entirely offline. No analytics, no advertising, no account login, and no network communication.

Data Storage

All settings (theme, reminder times, volume boost level) are stored locally on your device using Android’s built‑in storage (DataStore / SharedPreferences). This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions solely for its core functionality:

  • Notification permission – to show the playback controls and reminders.
  • Wake lock – to keep the audio playing when the screen is off.
  • Vibrate – for the reminder notification vibration.
  • Exact alarm – to schedule daily reminders.
  • Boot completed – to re‑schedule reminders after a reboot.

None of these permissions are used to collect data.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app contains only Quranic audio and does not display any harmful content or collect personal data.
